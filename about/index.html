About Todo App

Simple, secure task-management built for individuals and small teams who value focus over feature-bloat.

Stay Organized

Capture tasks in seconds, group them by project, and drag-drop to reprioritize—anywhere, on any device.

Data You Can Trust

Firebase Authentication & Firestore keep your data encrypted in transit and at rest—no compromises.

Lightning Fast

Built with modern technologies (Javascript, Tailwind, Flowbite) for sub-second interactions and real-time sync.

Under the Hood

  • •Front-end: HTML 5, TailwindCSS v4, Flowbite components
  • •Logic:  Modern Javascript
  • •Back-end: Firebase Auth & Firestore(real-time DB + serverless)
